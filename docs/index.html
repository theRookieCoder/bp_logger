How do I use BP Logger?

Follow the steps below:

  1. When you open the app for the first time, you have to login using your Google account 1
  2. After logging in for the first time, the app should automatically log in afterwords
  3. In the home page, you have to enter your diastolic and systolic values in the appropriate boxes
  4. Once you are done, click the blue button to store your BP values!

How do I access my BP values?

In the app:

  1. First, slide from the left edge of the screen to open the drawer
  2. Click on the Access file button
  3. A file will open up in your browser, Drive, or Sheets
  4. This file contains your BP values, and the date and time they were written

FAQ

Why do I need to sign in to Google?

BP Logger stores your information in a spreadsheet in your Google Drive so that you can access your information quickly and easily from anywhere. Hence, the app needs access to your Google Drive to write to the spreadsheet.

How do I export my BP values?

You do not have to 'export' your BP values because the spreadsheet that your BP values are written to is accessible to you. Simply open Google Drive, open the folder called BP Logger, and there should be a spreadsheet there called log. This file contains the date, time, diastolic, and systolic values.

Will there be statistics?

Currently, there is no statistics in the app. This feature is on my roadmap however so I might introduce it later.
